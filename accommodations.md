The Berkeley Lab Guest House is a comfortable, serene hotel located on the Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory campus, just uphill from UC Berkeley. There is a convenient and frequent <a href="https://commute.lbl.gov/service/shuttle-2/" target="_blank">LBL shuttle bus service</a> to the Ernest Orlando Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.
